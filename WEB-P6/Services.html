Redbull Surfing
A few hours drive north of the world's most isolated capital city, Perth, Michelle met up with local power couple Imogen Caldwell and Cortney Brown. Two incredible surfers who excel when the swell gets serious, the pair refuse to leave the region they grew up in, no matter how many far-flung, exotic locations they're despatched to by sponsors. With the perfect hosts by her side – and a cameo appearance from local legend Ry Craike – and a string of powerful Indian Ocean swells inbound, it wasn't long until Michelle got what she came for.
